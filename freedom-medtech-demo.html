Who We Are

The Management Engine Behind Your Growth

Freedom MedTech Management LLC (“Freedom”) is a Puerto Rico–based management organization that delivers comprehensive executive leadership, full operational control, and strategic direction to medical-technology distribution and service companies across the United States.

Freedom functions as the corporate management engine behind its partners—responsible for day-to-day decision-making, performance execution, and long-term growth strategy—providing the structure, accountability, and leadership capacity of an internal executive team.

Operating under Puerto Rico’s Act 60 Export Services Decree and in compliance with U.S. Internal Revenue Code Section 937 requirements, Freedom delivers the strategic direction, financial governance, and business infrastructure that enable its partners to scale efficiently while maintaining operational excellence and regulatory compliance.

What We Do

Comprehensive Strategic & Executive Management Services

Freedom provides comprehensive Strategic & Executive Management Services designed to optimize performance, strengthen vendor relationships, and ensure sustainable growth for its client companies.

Corporate Strategy & Planning

Develop, implement, and periodically update long-term corporate strategy, business plans, and commercial objectives.

Vendor & Manufacturer Management

Negotiate, execute, and manage vendor, supplier, and manufacturer agreements, including pricing arrangements and rebate programs.

Key Account Business Development & Expansion

Serve as executive sponsor and lead business development for key accounts, including stakeholder mapping, pipeline management, account conversions, and pricing negotiations within approved parameters.

Sales Execution Oversight

Design and implement sales strategies, establish territory quotas, monitor KPIs, and supervise overall salesforce performance.

Operational Governance

Establish inventory policies, logistics and distribution guidelines, compliance programs, and risk management protocols.

Technology & Process Development

Design, implement, and maintain proprietary systems and platforms to enhance operational efficiency and data capture.

Performance Monitoring & Reporting

Deliver quarterly performance reports, vendor scorecards, and financial analyses to the client’s board or designated officers.

Risk Management

Identify, assess, and mitigate strategic, operational, reputational, and regulatory risks associated with commercialization activities.
