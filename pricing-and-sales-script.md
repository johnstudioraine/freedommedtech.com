# Austin Mault — Package Pricing & Sales Script

---

## PRICING ESTIMATE

### Reference Point
- **Hoosier MedTech (hoosiermedtech.com):** $4,600 total
  - Full brand book / design system
  - Multi-page marketing website
  - Brand identity, typography, color system, component library
  - This was the most complex deliverable — it's the umbrella brand + a complete design system

### What We're Selling Today

| Site | Scope | Individual Price | Package Price |
|------|-------|-----------------|---------------|
| **SurgiSoft Solutions** | 7-section landing page — hero, problem statement, 4-stage workflow, 2 product cards, CTA, footer. Product-focused. More complex. | $3,200 | — |
| **Freedom MedTech** | 6-section landing page — hero, services grid, value props, CTA, footer. Service-focused. Slightly simpler. | $2,800 | — |
| **Bundle (both)** | Both landing pages, cohesive brand system across all 3 entities | — | **$5,200** |

### Pricing Logic
- Hoosier MedTech at $4,600 included a full brand book + website — the heaviest lift (brand strategy, design system, full site build)
- SurgiSoft is a focused product landing page but has more technical complexity (workflow stages, product architecture)
- Freedom MedTech is a cleaner services page, slightly less complex
- Individually they'd total **$6,000**
- Bundle discount of ~13% brings it to **$5,200**
- This feels right: two landing pages together cost slightly more than the full brand + website, which makes sense because they're two separate brands with distinct messaging, audiences, and positioning

### If He Pushes Back
- Floor price for the bundle: **$4,800** (20% off individual)
- Do NOT go below $4,800 — at that point you're undervaluing the Hoosier work retroactively
- Anchor to the $6,000 individual total, not the $4,600 Hoosier price

## SALES SCRIPT

### Opening (After Showing the Demos)

> "Austin, I want to show you what I put together. I built out demo landing pages for both SurgiSoft and Freedom MedTech so you could see the direction — not just hear me describe it."

*[Walk through both demos]*

> "SurgiSoft is positioned as the product brand — it leads with the problem surgical teams face, walks through the workflow, and introduces SurgiScribe and SurgiSync as the two core products. Freedom MedTech is the services arm — executive management, consulting, supply chain strategy. Two very different stories, two different audiences."

---

### The Pivot — Selling Both

> "Now here's what I want to talk through with you. I know how you operate — you like to focus, get one thing dialed, then move to the next. I respect that, and honestly that's how I'd want to build them too. One at a time, full attention, no split focus.

> But here's why I think it makes sense to **commit to both now**, even though we'll develop them sequentially:

> **1. Price.** Individually these would run $3,200 for SurgiSoft and $2,800 for Freedom — that's $6,000. If we lock both in today, I'll do the package at **$5,200**. That's $800 back in your pocket.

> **2. Brand cohesion.** Hoosier MedTech is the parent. SurgiSoft and Freedom are the two arms. If I build them together — even one after the other — I'm thinking about how all three brands talk to each other from day one. If we do them months apart, I'm reverse-engineering that cohesion instead of designing it in.

> **3. Momentum.** You've got three entities. Right now Hoosier has a site. SurgiSoft and Freedom don't. Every week without a landing page is a week where someone Googles your product or your services brand and finds nothing. We knock both out in sequence and your entire ecosystem has a digital presence."

---

### Handling "Let Me Just Do One First"

> "Totally get it. And to be clear — the build process IS one at a time. I'm not splitting my attention. We pick which one to start with, I build it, you review it, we launch it. Then we roll right into the second one.

> The only thing that changes is we're agreeing on the scope and price for both today, so you get the package rate and I can plan the brand system across all three from the jump.

> Which one do you want to lead with?"

---

### Closing

> "So the package is $5,200 for both — SurgiSoft and Freedom MedTech landing pages. We build them one at a time, your call on the order. I can get started this week.

> Want to lock it in?"

---

### If He Only Wants One

Don't fight it. Take the single site at full price ($3,200 or $2,800). Then after delivery:

> "Now that SurgiSoft is live, ready to knock out Freedom? I can still honor a discount if we move on it in the next two weeks."

This gives you a second close and keeps the relationship warm.
